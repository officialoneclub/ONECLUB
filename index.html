ONEGAME is a digital gaming project created with a simple but powerful vision: transforming playtime into real-world human support. Our mission is to turn every minute spent inside a game into a small opportunity to help people facing difficult living conditions around the world.

ONEGAME plans to dedicate 30% of the company’s future profits to international humanitarian causes and social support initiatives. During the first phase of the project, donations will support some of the world’s most recognized humanitarian organizations:

• UNICEF
• Save The Children
• Oxfam
• Médecins Sans Frontières
• Habitat For Humanity
• WWF



Every donation will be shared publicly through ONEGAME’s official Instagram page, where the community will be able to follow updates, view donation proofs, and participate in public polls regarding future support distributions. The community itself will play an active role in deciding how percentages are allocated between organizations.



The long-term vision of ONEGAME is far bigger than gaming. The project aims to contribute to the future development of prefab homes, support structures, and humanitarian assistance in regions affected by severe economic and social difficulties. Some of the areas ONEGAME hopes to support include:

• Africa — Burundi, South Sudan, Sahel
• India — Bihar, Uttar Pradesh
• America — West Virginia, Rust Belt
• Europe — Romania, Bulgaria, Greece



ONEGAME does not require any financial contribution from users. Revenue is generated exclusively through discreet, non-invasive advertisements displayed inside the gaming platform. No subscriptions. No mandatory purchases. Just play.

Every game session becomes a small chance to create something positive for someone else.

Thank you for being part of ONEGAME ♥️🎮
